Never Pawz LLC is my indie-game development company, currently focused on releasing its first project **[[Geode/Never Pawz/Website/BCV\|BCV]]** for iOS and Android. 
## Who
- My name is John Manning.  I'm a self taught developer, and most of my programing energy these past 5 years of learning and building have been in the Unity Game Engine.  
## Why
- I've always had a strong desire to create, and I wish I'd found game development when I was much younger.  Sometimes ideas bouncing around in your head can stay simple ideas, but not long ago an idea came to life and demanded I learn how to create and share it with others.  Attempting to create even the simplest of things adds a sense of awe and appreciation for all things created.
